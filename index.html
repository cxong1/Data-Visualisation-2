Wealth and Quality of Life

Cost of Living

The living cost around the world varies depending on countries. The cost of living index is a relative indicator based on the New York City with a base index of 100. If a country has an index of 50, this means that the cost of living in that country is only 50% that of New York City. This index will provide a clear overview and comparison of cost of living from country to country.

Cost of living continent map

The dot plot on the left shows the cost of different category of spending like rents, groceries, restaurant price and the cost of one Mcdonald meal We can observe that most country rent prices are lower than their purchasing power except for Singapore.
